Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in my ticket?

Your ticket includes access to all keynote and breakout sessions, workshops based on your tier, meals during conference hours, and a digital swag bag.

Is this event in-person, virtual, or hybrid?

DevConf 2026 is a fully in-person event at Moscone Center West. Recordings of select sessions will be made available afterward for Pro and Team ticket holders.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets are transferable to another attendee but non-refundable.

Do you offer student or group discounts?

Yes - students can apply for a discounted rate with a valid .edu email, and groups of 5+ receive a 15% discount on Standard and Pro tickets.
